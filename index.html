About Jeepney X

Supporting Cleaner and More Efficient Transport

Jeepney X Inc. is an EV-focused company established in November 2023 in Parañaque City, serving the growing need for cleaner and more efficient mobility solutions in the Philippines.

Our work combines technical capability and field support through battery supply, EV conversion, parts, and aftersales services for public utility transport stakeholders.
