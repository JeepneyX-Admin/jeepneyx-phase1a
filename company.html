Company Profile

Electric Mobility Solutions for the Philippines

Company Overview

Jeepney X Inc. is an EV-focused company established in November 2023, supporting the shift toward cleaner and more efficient transport solutions in the Philippines.

The company is based in Parañaque City, Metro Manila, and works with operators and partners through practical technical support and electric mobility services.
